Privacy Policy
How we protect your data at WB Employee Portal

Effective Date: June 2026

At the WB Employee Portal, your privacy and data security are our absolute highest priorities. Because our suite of tools deals with sensitive financial estimates and personal planning, we have purposely structured our platform to ensure complete confidentiality.

1. No Data Collection

Our calculators (including the Salary, Tax, Pension, 7th Pay Commission, and Increment tools) function entirely through client-side JavaScript. This means all mathematical calculations are performed locally within your own web browser on your own device. We do not collect, harvest, transmit, or store any of your numerical inputs, salary figures, or personal data on external servers or databases. What you type stays on your screen.

2. Local Storage

Certain tools on this website may utilize your browser's "LocalStorage" feature to temporarily save non-sensitive preferences (like keeping a dropdown menu selection active if you accidentally refresh the page). This data never leaves your personal device and can be cleared at any time simply by clearing your browser's history or cache settings.

3. Third-Party Analytics & Links

We may use basic, anonymized web analytics (such as counting total daily page visitors) to monitor site health and ensure our calculators are running smoothly. Furthermore, our website contains links to external news sources or official government circulars. Once you navigate away from our portal by clicking these external links, you are subject to the respective privacy policies of those third-party websites.

4. Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy as we add new tools, calculators, or features. Any changes to our data handling practices will be immediately and transparently reflected on this page.
