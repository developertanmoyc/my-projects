Quizlish - Master English Grammar

Welcome to Quizlish, the ultimate online platform designed for school students, competitive exam aspirants, and English learners to master English grammar and composition.

Quizlish is a lightweight, blazing-fast, single-page web application featuring dynamic quizzes, full-length mock tests, reading comprehension, writing samples, and a built-in AI Tutor powered by Google Gemini.
✨ Features

    📚 Comprehensive Grammar Topics: Practice specific sections including Prepositions, Tenses, Adjectives, Adverbs, Conjunctions, Voice Change, Narration Change, Joining, and Transformation.

    ⏱️ Full-Length Mock Tests: Dynamically generated 50-question mock tests pulled from a 1000+ question database, complete with a 35-minute countdown timer and a visual Question Navigator panel.

    🤖 Integrated AI Tutor: A built-in AI chatbot powered by Google Gemini 2.5 Flash. Ask questions about grammar rules, test answers, or writing tips directly from the interface. Includes smart rate-limiting (2 questions/minute).

    📝 Writing & Comprehension Section: Curated paragraphs on recent global topics (AI in Education, Green Economy, Space Exploration, Remote Work) to aid in reading and writing practice.

    🌗 Dark/Light Mode: Seamlessly switch between a bright, clean light theme and a sleek dark mode for comfortable night-time studying.

    📱 Fully Responsive: Built with Tailwind CSS, ensuring a perfect experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

    📊 Instant Feedback & Explanations: Immediate score calculation and detailed review sections explaining why an answer is correct.

    🔗 Social Sharing: Built-in WhatsApp and Facebook integration to challenge friends with your test scores.

🛠️ Tech Stack

    Frontend: HTML5, Vanilla JavaScript, Tailwind CSS (via CDN)

    Backend (Serverless API): Deno / Supabase Edge Functions (can also be adapted for Cloudflare Workers or Val Town)

    AI Integration: Google Gemini API (gemini-2.5-flash)
